HHH sources information about different legends in Hip-Hop culture. Navigate below to find important people that has made the genre to what it is today.

Kendrick Lamar

Born in '87, the young 33-year-old rapper Kendrick Duckworth Lamar has been regarded as one of the most influential artists of his generation, as well as one of the greatest rappers and lyricists of all time, ever since his mainstream debut in 2012 with good kid, M.A.A.D. city.









Kanye West

Kanye West, born in 1977, is an American rapper and record producer. West dropped out of highschool to pursue his music career in the Hip-Hop culture. He has been influential in the 21st-century development of mainstream hip hop and popular music in general. He is one of the world's best-selling music artists, with more than 20 million albums and 140 million singles sold worldwide.









JAY-Z

Being one of the world's best-selling music artists, with over 50 million albums and 75 million singles sold worldwide, JAY-Z is widely regarded as one of the most influential hip-hop artists in history, and often cited as one of the greatest rappers of all time.
